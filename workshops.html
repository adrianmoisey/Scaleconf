Workshops

With such a strong technical focus for the conference, we have decided to run workshops before the conference to give attendees hands-on experience with a number of toolchains.

Some of the workshops are free first-come-first-served hackathon-style workshops, where the engineers from various companies will be on hand to walk you through getting started on their products. Others are paid-for tutorials which will give you hands-on experience from experts in a specific topic.

Details for these workshops may change. If you are interested in attending workshops, please indicate on the signup form so that we can keep you updated as workshop plans get formalised.
