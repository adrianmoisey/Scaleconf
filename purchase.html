BUY TICKETS FOR SCALECONF

CONFERENCE

Dates: 18th-19th April 2013

Where: Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens

The early-bird fee for the conference is R1200.00 for a corporate ticket. If you are coming as an individual without the backing of your company, the early-bird cost is R1000.00.

What's included in your ticket:

  • 7 international speakers sharing unique experiences.

  • 5 African speakers sharing local perspectives and solutions.

  • Lunch every day, and tea/coffee/refreshments throughout the event.

  • Evening events every day to network and meet like minded people.

  • Free workshops, and time to build and hack with new technologies and people.

WORKSHOPS

We are going to be running workshops on the 17th and 20th of April. The details of the workshop have not been finalized yet, but when they are, we will email attendees with the relevant information.

For more information, see the workshops page.

PAYMENT INFORMATION

  • Student and Discount tickets

    There will be a pool of cheaper tickets available for students and individuals who are unable to afford the full price ticket. To qualify for one of these tickets, please write a brief letter of motivation to us at discounts@scaleconf.org. These tickets will be limited in supply and chosen at random three weeks before the event.

  • Payment Processor

    ScaleConf is using Quicket as our payment processor, and the organising beneficiary for ScaleConf is Lessfuss. If you would like more information on lessfuss, please visit lessfuss.co.za. If you have any problems at all with payment, please email info@scaleconf.org and a Lessfuss representative will be there to assist you.
